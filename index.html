Biography

I received the M.S. degree in electrical and information engineering from Nanjing University of Science and Technology, Nanjing, China, in 2016, and Ph.D. degree in School of Information Science and Engineering, Southeast University, Nanjing, China, in 2021, under the supervision of Luxi Yang. I was the recipien of the Outstanding Ph.D. Thesis Award of Chinese Institute of Electronics in 2021.

I have a strong background and track record in wireless communication theory and statistical signal processing estimation theory and have strong research skills including convex optimization theory, robust optimization theory, discrete optimization theory, and fractional optimization theory. I am now serving the editorial board of the IEEE Open Journal of the Communications Society, Digital Signal Processing, and Physical Communication.
